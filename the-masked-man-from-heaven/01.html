Nation of Fire and Water. The two neighboring kingdoms were formerly tranquil, but that is no longer true.

It all started when Doulos, a slave who had previously worked in both kingdoms, slew the Filya King of Fire, and Kalos King of Water. So that no one would suspect him, he made himself a king of fire and made Evdaimonia a queen in The Nation of Water.

Many people are unaware of what occurred to their former ruler, so his acts appear smooth.

On the one hand, the former queen of the fire has been dead for a long time, and Princess Fotia cannot succeed his parents as a leader due to restrictions.

On the other hand, the Water Prince's Gennaios vanished without a trace. Evdaimonia, his mother and the kingdom's queen, even sponsored a competition to find him.

The resurgence of Doulos, who now rules the fire nation, transformed the country into an authoritarian monarchy that significantly impacted the community, including the fire nation next door. To Doulos' delight, border trade is even monopolized.

Doulos, in particular, is a drinker who, even as a slave, enjoys imbibing large amounts of alcohol.
